Game Instructions

  • Move your mouse or finger to aim at the target position
  • Click or touch the screen to shoot bubbles
  • Shoot groups of the same color bubbles (3 or more) to eliminate them
  • Eliminate more bubbles to get higher scores
  • Game ends when bubbles reach the bottom
  • Complete each row to advance to the next level
